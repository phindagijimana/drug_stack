## What computation contributes

| Stage | Computational role |
| --- | --- |
| Preclinical PK/PD modelling | high — predicts human dose |
| Trial design | growing — adaptive designs, AI-driven enrolment |
| Biomarker discovery | high — omics, imaging, RWE |
| Patient stratification | growing — multi-omics + ML |
| Trial conduct | low — but RPA / NLP increasing |
| Pharmacovigilance | high — RWE + signal detection |

## Why this section is short

Clinical translation is a discipline with its own depth — biostatistics, regulatory affairs, clinical operations. This handbook covers what a computational scientist needs to **read** the clinical literature and **collaborate** with clinical teams, not how to design a phase III trial.

For depth, the right references are textbooks on clinical pharmacology and trial methodology.
